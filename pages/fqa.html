What materials are used in ClayofStories products?

ClayofStories products are crafted using high-quality stone clay and materials that are safe and durable. Each piece is carefully handcrafted to ensure exceptional quality and uniqueness.

How do I place a custom order with ClayofStories?

To place a custom order, simply reach out to us through our contact form. You can personalize colors, designs, sizes, and even add custom text or details to create a one-of-a-kind piece.

What is the production time for custom orders?

The production time for custom orders can vary depending on the complexity of the design and current workload. We will provide you with an estimated timeline when you place your order.

How should I care for my ClayofStories products?

We recommend handling your ClayofStories products with care. Avoid dropping them or exposing them to extreme temperatures or moisture. Use a soft, damp cloth for cleaning when needed.
